------- CSV/SQL Tables -------

1. Identification: id, project code

2. Geographical Information: id, country

3. Management and Fulfillment: id, managed by, fulfill via

4. Transaction Details: id, vendor inco term, shipment mode, po sent to vendor date, scheduled delivery date, delivered to client date, delivery recorded date

5. Product Details: id, product group, sub classification, vendor, molecule/test type ,brand, dosage ,dosage form, unit of measure (per pack)

6. Financial Information: id, line item quantity, line item value, pack price, unit price, weight (kilograms), freight cost (usd), line item insurance (usd)

7. Manufacturing Information: id, manufacturing site, first line designation
